How to select multiple polygons:
  • Click on a polygon to select it. The household data for all selected polygons will be shown below.
  • Click again on a selected polygon to deselect it.
  • You can select or deselect as many polygons as you want, one at a time, by clicking on them.
  • To clear all selections, click anywhere on the map background (not on a polygon).
  • Tip: The cursor will change to a pointer when you hover over a selectable polygon.
  • Long-press and drag on the map to draw a box and select all polygons inside the box.
